CPU Diagnostics

Possible Causes for CPU Test Failure:
  1. Bad electrical contact - Remove the CPU and reseat it to ensure good contact.
  2. Heat sink failure - Ensure conductive paste is present between CPU and heat sink.
  3. Fan failure - Ensure that CPU fan power is plugged in to motherboard.
